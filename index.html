The Moon Looked Pale Next to You

The moon once lit my quiet skies,
A faithful friend, a sweet surprise.

But then you came and in your glow,
The stars fell silent, time moved slow.

Your eyes, where dusk and fire meet,
Turned quiet nights to something sweet.

You spoke and all the world stood still,
As if my soul had found its will.

No silver light, no star above,
Can match the warmth within your love.

And now I stand, both brave and true…
The moon looks pale next to you.

So here I ask, with trembling heart,
Not just to end, but to restart.

Will you be mine, come joy or rain?
Let’s write a life beyond this pain.
